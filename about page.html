The Alawee manager is a salary payment platform. It makes payment of salaries of workers easy and automatic in an organization.

It is based on web3 and connected to the block chain network. Here the employer registers on the platform and enrolls all employees under his firm on the platform.

The employer registers with his crypto wallet and the employees also fill in their crypto address details. At a set date in the month for salary payment, the platform automatically credits the employee's crypto wallet the salary due from the employerâ€™s crypto balance. This is made possible by smart contract written into the platform.
